Who are we?
A Computer Science Club


We are the Cornell College computer science club. We strive to develop our skills and create a strong friendly environment. We host fun events like a annual hackathon, server-tour, and movie nights! We want to build a close community to hone our computer science skills where you can present your latest experiences and get healthy feed back from your peers!

Goals


Our number one goal is to enhance your .career opportunities. With our group projects and meetings going over job prep, this is a good place to develop new skills to add to your CS resume.

We are changing C4 to host a mindset of having a close .community of the computer science students at Cornell. We hope to develop lasting friendships and contacts for down the road.

We want you to have .fun! Computer Science has a poor stereo type of not being exciting. We are here to change that view; to show the joys of computer science though our events and projects!
